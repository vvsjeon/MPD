Motion Prior Distillation in Time Reversal Sampling for Generative Inbetweening

Wooseok Jeon, Seunghyun Shin, Dongmin Shin, Hae-Gon Jeon
Visual AI Lab, Yonsei University
ICLR 2026
Paper (PDF) OpenReview Code

Abstract

We propose Motion Prior Distillation (MPD), a training-free inference-time method that corrects motion bias in time-reversal sampling for generative inbetweening. MPD improves temporal consistency and reduces artifacts when generating intermediate frames between two keyframes using pre-trained video diffusion models.
